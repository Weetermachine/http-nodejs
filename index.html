Who I Am

Gary Leany

Local Business Owner and Resident of West Jordan for 25+ Years

Greetings, residents of West Jordan! I’m thrilled to introduce myself as a committed proponent of the Constitution. My campaign is deeply rooted in respect for this foundational document, ensuring our rights and liberties are always upheld. I believe in God, Country, Family, limited government, individual freedom, and economic prosperity. I support the right to life. I strive to ensure our city embodies these principles, fostering an environment of respect, responsibility, and opportunity. Together, we can strengthen our community while preserving the timeless values that make our nation great. I’m excited to engage with each of you as we shape West Jordan’s future in harmony with our cherished Constitution.

Priorities

West Jordan is a wonderful city to live in, and I strive to keep it that way for future generations.

We must defend our rights at the local level as well as the federal and state level.

The First Amendment provides that Congress make no law respecting an establishment of religion or prohibiting its free exercise. It protects freedom of speech, the press, assembly. These rights were violated over the last 2 years and must never happen again.

The 2nd amendment states The right of the people to keep and bear arms shall not be infringed; a well armed and well regulated militia being the best security. I believe that it is the best security of our country from foreign countries and is crucial to our remaining a free country.

Limited Government

Government closest to the people is the most responsive and effective form of government.

Fiscal Responsibility:

Saving Residents Money, and using our Taxpayers' Dollars to the Fullest

Public Safety & Law Enforcement:

Public safety is the primary role of government and a basic expectation of Taxpayers.

Transportation & Infrastructure

Our community has seen tremendous growth over the past decade. Growth, transportation and infrastructure impact us all, which is why these issues are important to me.

Communications & Transparency

I am committed to ensuring consistent communication with you. I believe in public accountability and transparency in government.
